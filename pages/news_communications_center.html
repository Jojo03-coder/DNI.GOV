BREAKING 2 hours ago • Director's Office

Director Gabbard Announces New Transparency Initiative for Declassified Intelligence Reports

The Office of the Director of National Intelligence today announced a comprehensive transparency initiative that will expand public access to declassified intelligence assessments and community reports. The initiative, part of Director Gabbard's commitment to government accountability, will establish new protocols for rapid declassification review and public release of intelligence products.

POLICY UPDATE 4 hours ago • Community Affairs

Intelligence Community Modernization Initiative Enters Phase Two

The Intelligence Community's comprehensive modernization program has successfully completed Phase One implementation and is now entering Phase Two, focusing on advanced analytics capabilities and inter-agency collaboration platforms. The initiative represents the largest technological upgrade in community history.

ACHIEVEMENT 6 hours ago • Public Affairs

Intelligence Community Receives Recognition for Cybersecurity Excellence

The National Security Agency and Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency have been jointly recognized by the Federal CIO Council for outstanding achievements in protecting critical infrastructure from cyber threats during the past fiscal year.
